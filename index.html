About Us

Everyday life is becoming increasingly fast-paced and stressful, especially in the city. We open our eyes to a shrill alarm, immediately reach for our phones, and the daily race begins. After a hectic day of constant running, we return home exhausted, craving the peace we all deserve.

Unfortunately, true relaxation is rare. Instead, we often walk into a cluttered room and yet another to-do list, leaving us irritated and fatigued. We desperately long for a quiet corner to find inner peace and claim a little "me-time."

Let us help you design the perfect green retreat.
Welcome to MySpace.

Our Vision

Harmony, Not Just Habit.

Nature provides everything we need to thrive—not just to survive, but to live beautifully. Yet, humanity has spent too long at odds with the environment. At MySpace, our vision is to bridge that gap. We believe it is our profound responsibility to stop polluting and start protecting. By choosing to live "in the green," we aren't just saving the planet; we are ensuring our own future. We are dedicated to nurturing the earth with the same love it gives to us.

Our Mission

We believe that every space, no matter how small or dull, has the potential to become a beautiful, relaxing retreat. Our mission is to breathe life into these concrete and barren spaces by transforming them into lush, green sanctuaries filled with vibrant plants, cozy seating, and a calming atmosphere.
